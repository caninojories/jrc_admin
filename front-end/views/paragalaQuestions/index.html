The COMMUNICATIONS' LEAGUE is the official organization of the Bachelor of Arts in Communication of Holy Angel University. The biggest event that organization holds is the PARAGALA: THE CENTRAL LUZON MEDIA AWARDS. This Year, PARAGALA will be on its second year and it aims to give recognation to the best media personalities, shows and TV station which are being aired throughout Central Luzon and in National Television.
